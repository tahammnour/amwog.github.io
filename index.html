About Amwog

A fast, simple and easy-to-use calling application

Amwog is a fast, simple and easy-to-use calling application that supports an interface for sent and received calls. It is characterized by the orange appearance of the call interface and the management of the call history such as deleting a call history for a contact and also adding new contacts from within the application with ease, an integrated communication application for those who do not like many misleading settings.
